The Power of Blood Donation

Empowering Lives: Donate Blood, Save Lives

Your contribution can make a significant impact on healthcare.

By donating blood, you become a lifeline for those in need. Your generosity ensures a steady supply of blood for medical emergencies, surgeries, and treatments. Here are the key benefits of blood donation:

  • Saves Lives: Donated blood is essential for accident victims, patients undergoing surgeries, and those with medical conditions requiring frequent transfusions.
  • Health Benefits: Regular blood donation can reduce the risk of certain health conditions and stimulate the production of fresh, healthy blood cells.
  • Community Impact: Your contribution strengthens the community's health and emergency response capabilities.

Eligibility Criteria for Blood Donation:

  • Age between 18 and 65 years
  • Minimum weight of 50 kg
  • Good general health
  • Adequate hemoglobin level
  • Not donated blood in the last 8 weeks

Join us in making a difference. Your blood donation can save lives and improve the well-being of countless individuals.
